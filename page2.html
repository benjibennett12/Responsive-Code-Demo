What is the Practical Use of Responsive Design?

Responsive design is highly beneficial for developer companies and web developers because it ensures that websites adapt seamlessly to various devices and screen sizes. This versatility leads to an enhanced user experience, reduced bounce rates, and increased engagement, ultimately boosting a site's performance and ranking.
